Ph.D. Student & Researcher

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at UIUC, where I am advised by Vikram S. Adve and Dilek Hakkani Tür. I also closely collaborate with Gokhan Tur and John F. Reid.

My research interests lie at the intersection of machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision. I am particularly interested in conversational generative agents that can interact with both the digital and physical world for real-world applications. I am part of the AIFARMS Institute, Center for Digital Agriculture, and the ConvAI Lab at UIUC.
